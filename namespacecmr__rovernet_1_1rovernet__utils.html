  logger

Helper function to convert a given motor command into a 40-byte encoding.

Current format, with number of bytes written in parantheses:

  • SUBTEAM (1): either 'drives', 'arm', 'astrotech', 'business'
  • MOTOR (1): either 'front_right', 'front_left', 'back_right', 'back_left'
  • POSITION (4)
  • DRIVES_VELOCITY (1)
  • DIRECTION (1): derived from DRIVES_VELOCITY
  • MAX_TORQUE (4)
  • MAX_VELOCITY (4)
  • MAX_ACCEL (4)
  • FEED FORWARD TORQUE (4)
  • EXTRA (20)
